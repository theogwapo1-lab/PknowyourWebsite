3. Filipino-Born Religious Movements
These are groups founded in the Philippines, often with unique doctrines.
A. Rizalian & Nationalist Religions

Various groups believing Jose Rizal was a prophet, messiah, or divine figure
Examples: Rizalista Religions, Ciudad Mistica de Dios, Bathalismo
